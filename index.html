Product Details

Our live lobsters are the real deal. Sourced directly from local fishermen in Maine, they represent the best of the North Atlantic. They are packed live and feisty, ensuring a restaurant-quality experience right in your own home. Sweet, succulent, and perfect for any occasion.

Your box contains your selected weight of live, wild-caught Maine lobsters (each ~1.25 lbs). They are packed in a secure, insulated box with eco-friendly gel packs to keep them cold and alive during their overnight journey.

For best results, cook your lobsters the day they arrive. If you must wait, do not remove them from the box. Place the entire sealed box in the coolest part of your refrigerator. **Do not** store them in water (fresh or salt) or on ice, as this will kill them.
